Why your feedback matters

YAPPL is a 3rd Year Project exploring how probabilistic programming can be made accessible and expressive. Your experience using the playground - what works well, what is confusing, and what you wish the language could do - helps tell me what needs to be changed.

Whether you found a bug, have a suggestion for a new feature, struggled to understand an error message, or simply want to share your thoughts, all feedback is appreciated (especially because it provides me with user acceptance testing to write about in my dissertation).

The form takes around 2–3 minutes to complete.

Open Feedback Form

The form opens in a new tab. I've limited the form to members of the University of Warwick, so you have to use your Warwick email to submit a response. There is also a chance your feedback is shared, however your identity will stay anonymous.
