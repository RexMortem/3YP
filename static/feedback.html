Why your feedback matters

YAPPL is a research language exploring how probabilistic programming can be made accessible and expressive. Your experience using the playground — what works well, what is confusing, and what you wish the language could do — directly informs how the language and tooling evolve.

Whether you found a bug, have a suggestion for a new feature, struggled to understand an error message, or simply want to share your thoughts, all feedback is genuinely valued and read. There are no wrong answers.
